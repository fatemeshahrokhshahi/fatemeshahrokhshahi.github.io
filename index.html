About

I am a Master's student in Computer Engineering at Istanbul Aydin University, graduating July 2026 with a 4.0 GPA. My research focuses on improving logical reasoning capabilities in large language models, with the goal of building more trustworthy and reliable AI systems.

I am particularly interested in overcoming the fundamental challenges that prevent current AI systems from reasoning correctly about conditionals, modals, and logical inference patterns. My work aims to bridge the gap between surface-level fluency and genuine logical understanding in language models.

Research Interests: Large Language Models, Logical Reasoning, Modal Logic, Trustworthy AI, Natural Language Processing, Prompt Engineering
